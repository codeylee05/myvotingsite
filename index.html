Student Presidential Elections

The official site for School House International Student annual Presidential Election for 2024

About

The annual Student Presidential Elections allow senior students of School House International to place their vote in the campaign of their choice
of which its president will run as the Student President for the year.
Students may find out more about individuals campaigns here on the site before placing a vote. Students are required to register and be validated before placing a vote. The Student Government Body title is awarded to the the campaign which recieves the most counts!
